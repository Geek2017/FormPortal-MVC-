About VoIP Billing Services

VoIP Billing Services is designed and built for Managed Service Providers to perform billing for telecommunications. VoIP Billing Services, serves as a billing platform and handles telecommunication taxes and fees to assure FCC compliance. Once your account is actively running traffic and set to Active Status, your account is deemed active to bill. After your account is placed in Active Status, your initial invoice will generate and be charged. Each month you will be charged on your Bill Date for your monthly service. I understand VoIP Billing Services will apply fee of $20.00 to my account if I Chargeback my monthly payment or if I reverse payment without VoIP Billing Service’s permission of any of the charges authorized on this form and I agree to pay this fee if this occurs.
